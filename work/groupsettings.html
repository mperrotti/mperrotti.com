Mobile Group Settings

  • Design
  • HTML prototype

As Meetup shifts towards being a mobile product, we continue bringing more over from desktop. The original desktop settings page was a confusing wall of links and text that accumulated clutter like some kind of junk drawer.

In order to make the settings page work on mobile, I first had to think about what settings were crucial to letting organizers run their groups, and which settings belonged somewhere more intuitive or no longer made sense.

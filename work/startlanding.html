Start Landing Page

  • Design
  • Illustration
  • Copywriting

Starting a Meetup group is a big decision for any user to make. I worked with Meetup's strategy team and usability lab to figure out what made people most uneasy about organizing a Meetup group, and designed a page to address those fears and let users move forward confident about the community they want to build.

Before this page existed, people were sent into the group creation flow with no information about what organizing a Meetup actually means, what Meetup does to help you build a community, and often not knowing that it costs money to become an organizer.

This page's content is often undergoing multivariate tests based on research about what makes people anxious about getting their group started so we can learn what messaging is relevant to a user who isn't sure whether or not they want to start a Meetup group.

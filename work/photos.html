The say "a picture is worth a thousand words", so one of the first desktop features we needed to bring to mobile was the ability to browse and comment on a Meetup group's photos and albums. Photos are one of the most engaging parts of browsing a Meetup group, but developing this feature was necessary to pave the way for future plans related to the redesign.

Even though the photos experience was designed and built to be responsive, the redesigned photo album experience is only launched on mobile web. Next year when we re-architect our front-end tech stack and we have a responsive Meetup group experience, this new design for looking through a group's photos will be launched across the entire web.

We quietly launched the new photos experience, and here's what we saw: the rate of people leaving Meetup after landing on a photo page drop dramatically and performing even better than our desktop photo pages, and the number of photos viewed went up, trending slightly higher than desktop.

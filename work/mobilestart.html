As we started to see more of our users shift to mobile platforms, we needed to design and build a simple flow to let people start their Meetup groups from their phone.

After looking at what steps people were dropping off at, I iterated on the design and built a responsive prototype that can be seen here

This flow is only on mobile web right now, but another designer is working on native iOS and Android designs, and replacing the desktop group start flow with a responsive start flow.

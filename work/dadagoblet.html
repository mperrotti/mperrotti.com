Crystal Goblet/Dada Manifesto

  • Design
  • Book binding

A juxtaposition of Beatrice Warde's essay, The Crystal Goblet, with Tristan Tzara's Dada Manifesto. The Crystal Goblet implores designers to set type in a disciplined way to honor the author's content and make the designers hand invisible in the typography. The Dada Manifesto, on the other hand, calls for radical self-expression.
